# DeutschFlow — Product Vision

**Version:** 1.0
**Last Updated:** 2026-07-11
**Status:** Active

---

## Vision

DeutschFlow will become the most effective and enjoyable way for complete beginners to learn German — combining structured CEFR-aligned curriculum, AI-powered conversation practice, and scientifically-proven spaced repetition in a single premium platform.

## Mission

Make German fluency accessible to everyone. We remove the friction of traditional language learning — no textbooks, no classroom schedules, no confusion about what to learn next — and replace it with a guided, motivating, and intelligent learning experience that adapts to each learner.

## Long-Term Goals (3-Year Horizon)

1. **Complete CEFR Curriculum** — Full A1 through C1 coverage with 100+ lessons, each with vocabulary, grammar explanations, dialogues, and exercises
2. **AI-Native Learning** — AI tutor that understands the learner's current level, adapts explanations, generates custom exercises, and provides real-time speaking feedback
3. **Mobile Applications** — Native iOS and Android apps with offline support for learning anywhere
4. **Community & Social** — Leaderboards, study groups, peer challenges, and shared progress
5. **Enterprise & Education** — Classroom management, teacher dashboards, and institutional licensing

## Product Philosophy

### 1. Guided, Not Gated

Every learner should always know what to do next. The path forward is always visible. Nothing is locked. Progress is celebrated, not used as a gate.

### 2. Serious, Not Sterile

Learning a language is an intellectual pursuit. The experience should feel premium, focused, and respectful — not gamified to the point of distraction. Motivation comes from visible progress, not cartoon rewards.

### 3. Explain, Don't Just Drill

Every exercise, every vocabulary item, every grammar concept should include an explanation of WHY. Learners should understand the language, not just memorize patterns.

### 4. AI as a Tutor, Not a Gimmick

The AI chat feature is a 24/7 language tutor — it explains concepts, corrects mistakes with explanations, roleplays scenarios, and adapts to the learner's level. It is not a chatbot. It is a teacher.

### 5. Progress Over Perfection

The spaced repetition system ensures learners review what they need, when they need it. Mastery is tracked, not gates. Every interaction builds toward long-term retention.

## Core Values

1. **Beginner-First** — Every feature, every UI element, every piece of content is designed for someone who knows absolutely no German.
2. **Evidence-Based** — Spaced repetition (SM-2 algorithm), CEFR-aligned progression, and research-backed teaching methods.
3. **Accessible** — WCAG 2.1 AA compliance, keyboard navigation, screen reader support, reduced-motion respect.
4. **Consistent** — One design language, one component library, one way to do things. No "different page, different rules."
5. **Continuous Improvement** — The product ships working features first, then polishes relentlessly.

## Success Metrics

- **Lesson Completion Rate:** % of started lessons that are completed
- **Daily Active Users (DAU):** Learners who engage with any feature
- **Retention:** % of users still active after 7, 30, and 90 days
- **SRS Efficiency:** Average interval growth (are cards moving to longer intervals?)
- **Quiz Accuracy:** Average score trends over time
- **Time to A1 Completion:** Median days from signup to A1 completion
- **NPS:** Net Promoter Score from in-app surveys
