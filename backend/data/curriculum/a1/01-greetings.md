# Erste Begegnungen — First Encounters

## Dialogue

*Read the conversation aloud:*

**Anna:** Hallo! Ich heiße Anna. Wie heißt du?
**Max:** Hallo Anna! Ich bin Max. Wie geht es dir?
**Anna:** Gut, danke! Und dir?
**Max:** Auch gut. Das ist mein Freund Thomas.
**Anna:** Hallo Thomas! Freut mich.
**Thomas:** Hallo Anna. Freut mich auch.

## Vocabulary

Practice the greetings and introductions vocabulary. Pay attention to the formal vs informal forms.

## Grammar: Personal Pronouns

In German, the pronoun 'I' is **ich**, and it's always lowercase (unless at the start of a sentence).

| Person | German |
|--------|--------|
| 1st singular | ich |
| 2nd singular (informal) | du |
| 2nd singular (formal) | Sie |
| 3rd singular (m) | er |
| 3rd singular (f) | sie |
| 3rd singular (n) | es |

**Sie** (formal 'you') is always capitalized.
